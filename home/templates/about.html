About

What is Project Veritas?

Project Veritas is an Artificial Intelligence tool built to help us understand more about what we read.

It has become very easy for us to share information with one another. We at Project Veritas think this is great! However, this free flow of information has also made it easier for some authors to mix less credible stories in with factual articles. Too much of what we can read and learn from on the internet is not always about figuring out what is true or sharing knowledge.

Instead of focusing on just the facts, some articles focus more on tapping into natural human reactions to how information is presented, not what the information is telling us. At Project Veritas, we want to enable readers to detect when an article is focused on credible information sharing vs. when the focus is on manipulating or influencing of the reader by means other than the facts.

Fear, anger, sensationalism don’t mix well with getting to the truth. The mission of Project Veritas is to empower the public with tools to isolate just the facts.

Who is Veritas?

Veritas is the Project Veritas' AI. Veritas has been trained to read the articles you send us and give you “Veritas’s opinion” on the article.

We trained Walt by letting it read millions of documents. These documents included journalism and scientific journal articles, satire articles, narrative fiction, opinion pieces, blogs, politically leaning news and even hate speech examples (Veritas did not enjoy reading the hate speech).

Veritas is still learning all the time. If you send ProjectVeritas an article and you think Veritas’s opinion wasn't quite right, please let us know, we will include that feedback as Veritas continues to learn and develop.
